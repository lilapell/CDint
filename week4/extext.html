EXCERPT FROM OLOGY MAGAZINE MANIFESTO

(2020)








"ART"

is the orgy lovechild of pounding ego and existential discomfort, of desperate throws to equate aesthetic taste with artistic talent, of disgustingly genuine attempts to say something and the untasteful concept that what we make has any real purpose in this world.


It is

ART,

yes.

But

ART,

at its purest, has never been that

ART.












"ART"

is an unnecessary.











ART

will not feed us,

ART

will not hydrate us, nor, will

ART

shelter us.






ART

is an unnecessary.

Yet still, we create. We let it linger.








ART,

at Her purest, will accept Her own meaninglessness, and She will embrace it. She 'll embrace it into

IRRATIONALITY;

She 'll embrace it into

DISCOMFORT;

She 'll embrace it into

UTTER FASCINATION

WITH

AMBIGUOUS MORALS

AND

INCESSANT EGOS

AND

MANIPULATED AUDEINCES




BECAUSE MEANINGLESS MEANS FREEDOM.
